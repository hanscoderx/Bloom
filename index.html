Yes! No. In fact, the course includes chapter that help you find the right product ideas, validate them, and even explore options like print on demand or digital products, ideal if you don’t have inventory yet.

Will I need to spend extra money on tools or software?

Not necessarily. We guide you through free and low-cost tools to get started. Some optional tools may be suggested for scaling, but they’re not required.
